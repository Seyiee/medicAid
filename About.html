About us

Medicaid Radio-Diagnostic Center is a wholly indigenous company that was established in 2009 and is dedicated to improving the standard of diagnostic services in Nigeria. It is the first of its kind private facility located in the heart of Abuja, the capital city of Nigeria and is born out of a passion to be the forerunner in the provision of radiological and diagnostic services.

Medicaid is a RADIO-DIAGNOSTIC CENTRE established in Nigeria with the aim of giving world class and accurate diagnosis always. We provide the highest standards of interventional, diagnostic and specialist services across the nation. Patients can receive a high standard and cost effective care and diagnosis in a comfortable environment.

Our services are retained by reputable organizations such as Nigeria National Petroleum Corporation (NNPC), Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN), Nigeria Deposit Insurance Corporation (NDIC), National Pension Commission (PENCOM), Department State Security (DSS), AGIP, GTB, ASO Savings and Loans, First Bank, Airtel, the Nigerian Police and numerous other private and government bodies.
